Frequently Asked Questions

Here is some questions that people asked frequently

What is GlucoGuide and how does it work?

GlucoGuide is a health tracking platform where users can input their daily glucose and blood pressure levels into a calendar. The system analyzes the data to predict potential health issues and provides dietary suggestions based on the trends.

How do I track my glucose and blood pressure on GlucoGuide?

Simply log in to your account, navigate to the calendar, and enter your daily glucose and blood pressure readings. The system will automatically analyze the data and offer insights.

Can GlucoGuide help predict health issues related to diabetes and hypertension?

Yes, GlucoGuide uses your input data to monitor trends and can predict potential risks, helping you take preventive measures based on your readings.

How accurate are the dietary suggestions provided by GlucoGuide?

The dietary suggestions are based on your personal data and common nutritional guidelines to manage glucose and blood pressure. However, it's always recommended to consult a healthcare professional for personalized advice.

Is my data safe on GlucoGuide?

Yes, we take your privacy and data security seriously. Your information is stored securely and only accessible to you.

Can I share my health data with my doctor?

Yes, GlucoGuide allows you to export your health data, which can then be shared with your healthcare provider for further consultation.

How often should I update my glucose and blood pressure readings?

For the best results, we recommend updating your readings daily to get accurate predictions and tailored dietary advice.

Can GlucoGuide help manage both high and low glucose levels?

Absolutely! Whether you're managing high blood sugar or low, GlucoGuice offers suggestions and insights tailored to your specific health needs.

Does GlucoGuide support multiple users or family tracking?

At this time, GlucoGuide is designed for individual users. Future updates may include family or multi-user tracking options.
